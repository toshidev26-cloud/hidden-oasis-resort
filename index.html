Welcome to the Oasis

A hidden gem, far from the noise

Tucked away on Naranjita Street in Gingoog City, Hidden Oasis Resort is a tranquil retreat designed for travelers who crave peace, nature, and genuine comfort. Lush gardens, a refreshing pool, and a cozy on-site café bar make it the perfect place to slow down and breathe.

With just nine thoughtfully appointed rooms, every stay feels personal. Our multilingual team is here to make you feel at home, whether you're escaping the city for the weekend or chasing waterfalls with the whole family.
